# Brand Vault: Research Preferences

Configure your research style, quality standards, and output preferences.

---

## Research Style

**Citation format:** APA

**Formality level:** Varies by project context (adjust based on audience and deliverable type)

**First-person usage:** Yes (first-person is acceptable when appropriate to the context)

**Depth vs breadth:** Varies by research needs (deep dive for focused questions, broad survey for exploratory work)

---

## Quality Thresholds

**Source credibility:** Open to all source types, but choose judiciously—validate claims, corroborate across sources, and ground assertions in evidence

**Recency preference:** Context-dependent—prioritize latest sources for AI and fast-moving topics; older sources acceptable for stable, established fields

**Minimum sources per question:** 5

---

## Output Defaults

**Default format:** Varies by deliverable needs (report, briefing, dataset, documentation)

**Section style:** Numbered

**Include executive summary:** Yes

**Include methodology appendix:** Yes

---

## Publishing

**Auto-publish to Google Drive:** No (default unless explicitly enabled per project)

---

## Neurodivergent-Friendly Guidelines

These formatting preferences ensure output is accessible and scannable:

- **Executive summaries first**: Place key takeaways at the top so readers can quickly grasp main points
- **Clear, numbered sections**: Use consistent numbering for easy navigation and reference
- **Scannable structure**: Use bullet points, headers, and visual breaks to reduce cognitive load
- **Up-front conclusions**: Lead with findings, then support with details
- **Concise paragraphs**: Keep blocks of text short and focused
- **Bold key terms**: Highlight important concepts for quick scanning
- **Consistent formatting**: Maintain predictable patterns throughout documents

## Experiment Policy

**Default Behavior:** Every research session runs 1 AUTO experiment. This is how improvements accumulate.

**Experiment Types:**

| Type | Behavior | Approval |
|------|----------|----------|
| **AUTO** | Runs every session | Never needed |
| **LOW-RISK** | Runs by default, can opt out | Not required |
| **HIGH-RISK** | Explicit request only | Required |

**Skipping Experiments:** Use `research strict [topic]` to skip all experiments (for baselines, time-sensitive work)

**Graduation:** After 3 runs with avg +3 RQS improvement, AUTO experiments graduate to "standard practice"

**Logging:** All experiments must be logged in `IMPROVEMENT-LOG.md` with:
- Experiment ID
- RQS before/after
- Change (Δ)
- Keep/Discard decision

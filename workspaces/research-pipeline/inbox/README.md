# Research Inbox

Drop research topics, questions, or discoveries here for the research-pipeline agent to process.

## How It Works

1. **Drop files here** - Any markdown file with research ideas, questions, or discoveries
2. **Agent processes** - When working with research-pipeline agent, it reads this inbox
3. **Sort together** - Decide what to research, what to archive, what to defer

## File Naming

```
YYYY-MM-DD-topic-slug.md
```

Example: `2026-03-12-cognitive-bias-bug-dismissal.md`

## What to Drop Here

- Research questions that came up during other work
- Discoveries that deserve deeper investigation
- Topics you want to explore later
- Half-formed ideas that need development

## What NOT to Drop Here

- Completed research (goes to `archive/`)
- Raw external content (goes to `stages/00-monitor/output/raw-*.md`)
- Notes about other workspaces (keep in those workspaces)
